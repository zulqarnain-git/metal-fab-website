About MetalCraft Pro

Crafting excellence in metal for over two decades

Founded in 2002, MetalCraft Pro has grown from a small welding shop to a full-service metal fabrication company serving clients across multiple industries. With a team of highly skilled fabricators, welders, and engineers, we combine traditional craftsmanship with cutting-edge technology to deliver exceptional metalwork solutions.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest CNC machinery, allowing us to tackle projects of any size and complexity with precision and efficiency. We pride ourselves on our attention to detail, commitment to quality, and dedication to meeting our clients' exact specifications.
